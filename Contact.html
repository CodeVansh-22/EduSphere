Frequently Asked Questions

What is EduSphere?

EduSphere is an online platform offering free and paid courses in programming, design, and more. Learn anytime, anywhere!

How do I enroll in a course?

Visit the Courses page, select your preferred course, and click "Enroll Now". You’ll be guided through the process step-by-step.

Do I get a certificate after completion?

Yes, upon completing a course, you’ll receive a digitally signed certificate which can be downloaded or shared on LinkedIn.

Is EduSphere free?

EduSphere offers both free and premium courses. Many foundational topics are free to access without any payment.
